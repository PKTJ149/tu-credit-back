# Learning and Registration UX Copy Draft v1

## Purpose

This document provides first-pass UX copy for the learning/registration redesign.

It is intended as working draft content for:

- wireframes
- high-fidelity design
- prototype reviews

## Voice

- Clear
- Encouraging
- Academic
- Practical
- Forward-looking

## Learning Goals

### Title

- `Learning goals`

### Body

- `Explore recommended learning paths, track your progress, and decide what to study next.`

### Actions

- `View program`
- `View subject`

### Empty State

- `No recommendations are available yet. Explore programs and subjects to get started.`

## Program / Subject Decision View

### Title

- `Review this learning option`

### Body

- `Check the details below to see whether this option is the right next step for you.`

### Actions

- `Register`
- `Save for later`

## Registration Review

### Title

- `Review registration`

### Body

- `Confirm the details below before you complete your registration.`

### Supporting Labels

- `Selected item`
- `Credits`
- `Requirements`
- `Payment summary`

### Actions

- `Confirm registration`
- `Edit selection`

## Registration Confirmation

### Title

- `Registration complete`

### Body

- `Your registration has been recorded successfully.`

### Payment Required Variant

- `Payment is required before this registration can move forward.`

### Actions

- `Go to my registrations`
- `Go to finance`

## My Registrations

### Title

- `My registrations`

### Body

- `Review your active and recent registrations below.`

### Empty State

- `You don't have any active registrations yet.`

### Finance Callout

- `Payment is still required for one or more registered items.`

### Actions

- `View registration`
- `Go to finance`

## Academic Progress

### Title

- `Academic progress`

### Body

- `Review your completed learning record, credits, and results.`

### Empty State

- `Your academic record will appear here once results are available.`
