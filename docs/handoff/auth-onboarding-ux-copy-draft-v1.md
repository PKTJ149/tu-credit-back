# Auth and Onboarding UX Copy Draft v1

## Purpose

This document provides first-pass UX copy for the auth/onboarding redesign.

It is intended as working draft content for:

- wireframes
- high-fidelity design
- prototype reviews

## Voice

- Clear
- Calm
- Official
- Supportive
- Direct

Avoid:

- technical jargon
- bureaucratic wording
- copied legacy terminology

## Login

### Page Title

- `Sign in to CREDIT BANK`

### Supporting Copy

- `Access your learning account to manage registrations, payments, and progress.`

### Field Labels

- `Email or National ID`
- `Password`
- `Remember this device`

### Primary CTA

- `Sign in`

### Secondary Actions

- `Forgot password?`
- `Create an account`
- `Sign in with Google`

- `Enter your email or national ID.`
- `Enter your password.`
